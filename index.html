About

I am a dual-degree student pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and MS by Research in Computational Linguistics at IIIT Hyderabad. My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing, multimodal reasoning, and AI safety.

Currently, I work with Prof. Ponnurangam Kumaraguru on fairness in Federated Learning and privacy risks in personalized AI agents. I also collaborate with Prof. Manish Shrivastava on model compression and non-autoregressive language generation. Previously, I worked with Prof. Dan Roth (UPenn) and Prof. Vivek Gupta (ASU) on evaluating vision-language models for chart and map understanding.
